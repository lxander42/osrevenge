OSRevenge

Open Source Reverse Engineering Database for Mechanical Parts

Wiki

OSRevenge is a free and open source reverse engineering database for mechanical parts. Our mission is to create a community-driven platform where engineers and makers can collaborate and share knowledge about the design and manufacturing of mechanical parts.

Search and Contribute to the Database

With OSRevenge, you can search for parts, view their specifications and schematics, and even contribute to the database by sharing your own designs and reverse engineering projects.

Get Involved

If you're interested in contributing to the OSRevenge community, we welcome you to join our forum, where you can discuss projects, ask questions, and share your knowledge with other members.
